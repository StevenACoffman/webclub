A Story of a Thanksgiving Dinner

Once there live an old retired hunter and his wife who were going to host a thanksgiving dinner party this year for their children and grandchildren. Everything was set and ready except the turkey. It was only and hour until the guests were to arrive, and they had no turkey. Now, this couple lived far out in the country, away from any stores, so they couldn't just buy a turkey. Finally, they decided that the man should go out and hunt for one. The man did so, but was not able to come across any turkeys. All he could find was a fat little bird. He sadly brought the bird home to his wife, who cooked it and spiced it, knowing there was no other choice. When it dinner began, the whole family feasted as merrily as ever. The hunter brought out the so-called turkey, and to his suprise, everyone loved it! They said it was the most delicious turkey they had ever tasted! And it was the hunter's and the hunter's wife's little secret that the most delicious turkey ever was really no turkey at all.

The End!
